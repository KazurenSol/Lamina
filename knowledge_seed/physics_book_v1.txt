Chapter 1: Motion Basics

Velocity is displacement over time.

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity.

Force is mass times acceleration.

Mass is the amount of matter in an object.

Force depends on mass and acceleration.

Acceleration depends on velocity.

Chapter 2: Energy Basics

Kinetic energy is energy of motion.

Potential energy is stored energy due to position.

Energy is the capacity to do work.

Kinetic energy depends on mass and velocity.

Potential energy depends on mass, gravity, and height.
