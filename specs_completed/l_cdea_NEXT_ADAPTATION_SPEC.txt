PROJECT: L-CDEA
MODULE: adaptation/

PURPOSE:
Enable discovery and validation of new encodings, compression schemes, and algorithmic backends.

---

# DIRECTORY STRUCTURE

l_cdea/adaptation/
├── encoding_discovery.py
├── compression_candidates.py

├── benchmark_runner.py
├── validator.py

└── versioning.py

---

# FUNCTION

Observe repeated patterns
→ Propose new encoding/algorithm
→ Benchmark
→ Validate
→ Register (if approved)

---

# VALIDATION RULES

1. Deterministic
2. Lossless unless declared
3. Improves cost (memory or compute)
4. Type-safe
5. Reversible or traceable
6. Versioned
7. Does not alter CDL semantics
8. Passes benchmarks

---

# CORE RULE

Encodings MAY optimize storage/execution  
Encodings MUST NOT change meaning

END OF SPEC
